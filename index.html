CEE Information

Medical Education Common Entrance Examination

Critical information every Nepal medical aspirant must know before attempting CEE.

Eligibility Criteria

Minimum 50% or 2.4 GPA in 10+2 (Science) with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology, or equivalent.

Exam Format

Single paper containing 200 Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs) to be completed in 3 hours.

Mark Distribution

  • Physics: 50
  • Chemistry: 50
  • Botany: 40
  • Zoology: 40
  • Mental Agility Test: 20
